To Those Who Work In Acres, Not In Hours

Our vision is to promote uniformity in agriculture marketing by streamlining of procedures across the integrated markets, removing information asymmetry between buyers and sellers and promoting real time price discovery based on actual demand and supply.

Understand Learn Use

We believe that each farmer in our country is capable of producing lot more cultivation by our own traditional approach , just proper education is important for it , for which we provide farming tutorials. To make maximum out of it we have also provided guidelines to help farmers and users understand website in detail.

Your Farm Our Technology

Our motive is to integration of APMCs across the country through a common online market platform to facilitate pan-India trade in agriculture commodities, providing better price discovery through transparent auction process based on quality of produce along with timely online payment.

Apna Market Apna Kisan

This section consists of different farmer related profiles with each profile stating the farmers personal details , products available and feedback-rating.The user can directly select a profile or can filter them on basis of location of farmer or product.After selecting a profile user can go through the price of the products demanded by farmer , add to cart the products and finallypay via payment gateway.

Apna Wholesale Apna Kisan

This section consists of different wholesaler related profiles with each profile stating the wholesalers personal details , products needed and feedback-rating.The user can directly select a profile or can filter them on basis of location of wholesaler or product.After selecting a profile user can go through the buying price of the products put-forward by wholesaler , select the products and choose a payment option.

Apna Kisan Apna Samman

Here we focus on providing different employment opportunities for the position as workers in farms and retail shops. The employers profile will display the work details, accommodation , duration and pay details (optional) users can select an employer profile or filter by location of employer and position. After selecting a profile user can chat with the employer.

Har Ghar Kisan

This section consists of transport service providers, each providers profile will be displayed with personal details and pay (optional) .The users can directly select a profile or filter on the basis of location of transporter. After selecting a profile users can chat with the transporter. Users have to pay basic price for confirming booking via payment gateway.
